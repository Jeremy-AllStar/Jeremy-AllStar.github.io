About Me

My name is Jeremy and I am from Bronx, New York. My parents are also from Ghana making me a proud African American. I enjoy doing lots of things like coding different languages, playing sports, and just having a great time with friends.... I enjoy doing these things because these are what make me the happiest no matter what I am feeling. In the near future I want to find accesss to many internships so I can pursue my dream to become an eletrical engineer. The reason for this is because I want to be a successful black man when I grow up and in order to do this I know that I will need to work hard as a kid so it can pay off in the future. Although some may make the case that racism has ended, I do not believe it because people are still being mistreated even as you read this, I want to achieve a dream of becoming a man who can overcome these challeneges.
