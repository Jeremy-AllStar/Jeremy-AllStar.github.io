Portfolio

At my school we were required to make a 3D model of our campus in around three months. To complete this project my group and I used drones to take pictures of the buildings from different angles and uploaded these pictures to a program called MeshMixer.... In MeshMixer we were required to change the pictures into a 3D model, clean up the object, and fill in any holes. After the 3D models were finalized, we saved them as .STL files so that our 3D printing software would recognize it. Utilizing Prusa Slicer, we scaled it down to size and officially printed our school campus.
